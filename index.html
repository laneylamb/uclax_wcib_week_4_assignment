Why #BrewHonu

Hawaiian Green Sea Turtles, or honu, are a symbol of good luck in Hawaiian culture. These important and adorable creatures continue to be under threat, but you can help! We donate 10% of the proceeds from your purchase to turtle conservation efforts so you can save with every sip.

Get to know a few of our honu. Meet Cruiser.

Divers found him tangled in fishing nets, struggling to survive. He was nursed back to health at Maui Ocean Center and released just a few weeks later.

Placeholder

Say hello to our smallest save. Meet Rambo.

This little guy is as fierce as his namesake! As he crawled to the ocean shortly after hatching, a bird grabbed him, but Rambo managed to break free. Maui Ocean Center biologists cared for his wounds and gradually reintroduced him to the sea.

Placeholder

And finally, our biggest save. Meet Yoda.

This big fella was found stuck in a freshwater pond. Luckily, experts from the Marine Turtle Research Program skillfully coaxed the 305-pound animal out of the pond and after a thorough examination, released him into his natural habitat.
